Scam 1992: The Harshad Mehta Story is an Indian Hindi-language crime drama web-series on SonyLIV directed by Hansal Mehta, with Jai Mehta serving as the co-director.
Based on 1992 Indian stock market scam committed by stockbroker Harshad Mehta, the series is adapted from journalist Sucheta Dalal and 
Debashish Basu's 1992 book The Scam: Who Won, Who Lost, Who Got Away.
The screenplay and dialogues were written by Sumit Purohit, Saurabh Dey, Vaibhav Vishal and Karan Vyas.
